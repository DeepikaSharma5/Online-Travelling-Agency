Sri Lanka Top Beaches
Tour Package (8D/7N) Details

This special, winter peak season tour package, gives you access to all the top beaches on Sri Lanka's South and South-West Coast. No need to break it down into surf beaches, snorkeling & scuba beaches, whale watching beaches, party beaches, romantic beaches, secret beaches or just outstanding relaxing beaches. Here you got it all in one. Just pick your beach/activity for the day and your private driver/car will take care of the rest.

Naturally we will be happy to customize this special Sri Lanka Tour Package according to your dates and requirements.

SRI LANKA TOP BEACHES ITINERARY (8 days / 7 nights)

Day 1 : We will meet you at your arrival at the Bandaranaike International Airport and drive you to your first Beach Hotel in Negombo. Depending on your arrival time you can undertake a sightseeing tour of the town, the fishing harbour and the colonial monuments (included in the price and private) or just relax at the hotel pools, take a dip in the Indian Ocean and enjoy a truly tropical sunset.

Day 2 - 5 : After breakfast you will be leaving for Hikkaduwa, passing Colombo on your way (optional sightseeing/Shopping).
As one of the most beautiful beaches on Sri Lanka's coastline, Hikkaduwa is adored for its liveliness and diversity. A haven for water sports enthusiasts and suitable for individuals, couples, families and friends, and even for those who want to swim in the breaking waves, feel the pure sea breeze and touch the warm sun rays. You have the opportunity to go on sea boat rides, surfing, snorkelling and scuba diving. The Coral Sanctuary, the most popular attraction of Hikkaduwa beach city, is just a five minutes’ walk away from the hotel.
If you are looking for diversity we recommend the following half-day/day tours from Hikkaduwa:

  • Historical Galle
  • Unawatuna
  • Madhu Ganga River Safari
  • Bentota Beach
  • Turtle Hatchery
  • Hikkaduwa Bird Lake / Dodanduwa Lake

Day 5 - 7 : Your hotel in Ahangama / Koggala is set amidst the swaying coconut palms and the famous waters of Koggala Lake, dotted with islands. A stone-throw away you find the pristine beaches of Ahangama. Ahangama is still a "secret" destination visited mostly by surfers and “insiders” looking for a place to relax away from the crowds.
Recommend half-day/day tours from Koggala:

  • Visit the Buddhist hermitages situated on a couple of islets on the Koggala Lake
  • Whale Watching in Weligamma
  • Surfing & Partying in Mirissa Beach
  • Yala National Wildlife Park
  • Sunset Cruise on the Koggala Lake
